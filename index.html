Fantasy FB Emailer
Participating in multiple fantasy football leagues, I wanted to create a way of emailing league members with info and stats on the last week performances, the standings, and the top/worst performers. Written in python, this application utilizes BeautifulSoup to scrape league info from ESPN, then formats it into an HTML email and sends it to all league members.
